Challenge Overview

Dive deep into the competition details, understand the scope, and prepare for success.

Challenge Context

You're stepping into the shoes of strategic consultants for Aurora Labs, a cutting-edge AI startup seeking to expand into new markets. The company has developed breakthrough machine learning algorithms but faces complex decisions about market entry, product positioning, and competitive strategy. Your team will analyze market data, customer insights, and competitive landscapes to craft a comprehensive go-to-market strategy.

Expected Impact

Success in this competition means developing actionable insights that could genuinely influence Aurora Labs' strategic direction. Winning teams will demonstrate measurable impact through revenue projections, market penetration strategies, and risk mitigation approaches. Your recommendations should address scalability, sustainability, and competitive advantage in the rapidly evolving AI landscape.

Solution Framework

Teams will receive comprehensive data packages including market research, financial statements, customer surveys, and competitive analysis. Your solution should encompass market sizing, customer segmentation, pricing strategy, and implementation roadmap. Focus on data-driven insights, innovative approaches, and practical execution plans that Aurora Labs can realistically implement within 18 months.

Your Role & Expectations

As a competitor, you'll work in teams of 3-4 members, combining diverse perspectives and skills. Each team receives mentorship from industry professionals and access to exclusive resources. You'll have 48 hours to analyze the case, develop your strategy, and prepare a compelling presentation. Final presentations will be judged by senior executives from top consulting firms and tech companies.
